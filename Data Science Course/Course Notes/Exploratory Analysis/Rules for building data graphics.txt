Principle 1: Show comparisons
-Evidence is always relative to another competing hypothesis
-Show a comparison in a plot Control vs Experimental

Principle 2: Show causality, mechanism, or explaination
-How you believe the world works

Principle 3: Show multivariate data, show as much data on a single plot as you can.
-integrate alot of data on a plot.

Principle 4: Integration of evidence
- don't let the tools drive the analysis
- make as information rich as possible

Principle 5: Describe and document evidence with labels and sources

Principle 6: Content is king
